6 months in Hong Kong

Who are we?

Sophoclis

I went to Hong Kong for the first time in July 2017, to do a 6-month internship in a French consulting company. After one week, I was already hooked on this city. Hong Kong is simply fascinating and it’s impossible to get bored with all the activities you can do. One of my best life experiences so far.

Tristan

I went to Hong Kong on an academic exchange, as part of my 3rd year. The experience was incredible. Meeting people from all over the world, discovering a new country, a new culture made those 6 months unforgettable. When Hong Kong catches you it's hard to run away.

Alexis

I went to Hong Kong for the first time in 2017 to work for a French media company. From the beginning I was excited by the dynamism of Hong Kong. I had a very fast pace of life. Hong Kong people follow the norm that "time is money" and "efficiency is the key to success". That means that you walk fast, you eat fast, you speak fast, and live fast.

What about Hong Kong?

Hong Kong,

Its name inspires visions of a chaotic, jam packed city with soaring skyscrapers, thick smog, endless noodle stands, big finance, and wild nights out. A busy city of eight million inhabitants with one of the biggest hub airports in the world. It can be overwhelming for many visitors, especially those not used to crowded places. And, with so much to do here, one can scratch one’s head about where to start in order to get the most out of the trip.

Huge nights out!

Monday nights in a local bar, tuesday night in a roothtop bar with a view all over Hong Kong and its bay, a Wednesday watching horseraces and finishing in Wan chai, a thursday night camping on the beach, and to finish the week a few crazy nights in Lan Kwai Fong... Hong Kong parties go on 24/7, and suprise you all the time!

Weird food...

From chickens feet to fish balls, Hong Kong offers to its visiters a large range of local dishes, all more suprising than the previous one. When walking past different street food stands, you might feel brave and want to give one of the local specialities a go... We all did it but unfortunately rarely had a good suprise...

Amazing activites!

Hong Kong is full of suprises and breathtaking activities! Hong Kong suits everyone, offering some of the best hikes in the world for those who like the nature, on the way you can find amazing cliff jumping for those who prefer more extreme activites! For those who want to learn more about the culture, Hong Kong is full of History with some incredible temples and historical places!

Going back home with lifetime memories...
