Exploring Hong Kong

Hong Kong,

Is an incredible city in wich you can get lost extremely easily even after living there for 6 months, making it scary and so special at the same time.
Obviously 6 months is not enought to discover all Hong Kong secrets. But we thought we could sum up the must do's so that, if you ever have the chance to visit Hong Kong you will know where to go!

1. Eating at Mister Wongs

Eating at Mister Wongs is a must do when visiting Hong Kong! For 60 HK$, equivalent to 6 euros, you have unlimited beers and food in a room full of students all there to have a great time, creating an incredible and unique atmosphere! You serve yourself with beer directly in 2 huge fridges with around 600 beers every night!

2. Hiking to Lions rock

When talking about best views of the city of Hong Kong, people tend to say that Victoria peak is the best view over Hong Kong. However after living 6 months in Hong Kong, we all agree that the best view is from Lions rock. To get to the top there are no cable cars or roads, the only solution is hiking threw the jungle and the monkeys! Another incredible experience with one of the best sunsets in the world...

3. Visiting Lantau Island

The bid Buddha is a must do when visiting Hong kong. The Bronze statue has a heigh of 34m and in on the top of a mountain making it look even bigger! Big Buddha is located on Lantau Island so plan some time to visit some on the famous Honk Kong fishing villages on your way back to the city!

4. Street walking

In the end, the best way to explore this city is just to get lost in it. Walking around the city is the best way to understand how crazy and amazing Honk Kong is. The goal is just to get lost in the busy roads of Hong Kong and explore the hundreds of street markets Hong Kong offers and the dozens of temples all around the city...

Most used Transports

TAXI

3 types of taxis exist in Hong Kong depending on where you are located. You can diferentiate them by their color. A good but often slow way to move around Hong Kong.

BUS

The busses are on all night and deserve the most places in and outside town. It is often the best way to go to less popular places.

MTR

The MTR is Hong Kongs metro, it is one of the best in the world and is deffinetely the best way to move around the city.

TRAM

The tram is not used as much as the over transports but it is a very fun way to expore the city. You can also book them to party all night while moving around the city!

FERRY

The ferry is used a lot in Hong Kong to go from one island to another. A great way to see a beautiful view of Hong Kong! You can also rent boats to do a huge boat party!

Hong Kong climate

Hong Kong's climate is sub-tropical, tending towards temperate for nearly half the year. During November and December there are pleasant breezes, plenty of sunshine and comfortable temperatures. Many people regard these as the best months of the year. January and February are cloudier, with occasional cold fronts followed by dry northerly winds. Hong Kong has a special climate with frequent typhons and a very high humidity.
